## p349 43

$$
\begin{align}
a_{n} &= 6a_{n-1} - 8a_{n-2} + 3 \\
r^{n} &= 6r^{n-1} - 8r^{n-2} \\
r^{2}-6r+8 &= 0 \\
r &= 2, 4
\end{align}
$$

Since neither root is equal to 1 and 3 is a constant, we know the solution is in the form

$$
a_{n} = D
$$

$$
\begin{align}
D = 6D - 8D + 3
\end{align}
$$
