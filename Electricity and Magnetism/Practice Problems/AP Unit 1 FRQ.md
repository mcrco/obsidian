$$
\begin{align}

$$

$$

$$
\begin{align}

\implies E &= \frac{Q_{enc}}{A\epsilon_{0}}\\

Q_{enc} &= Q - 2Q  \\
&= -Q \\
\implies E &= \frac{-Q}{2\pi rL}
\end{align}
