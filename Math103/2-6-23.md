# Problem 1

Calculate $AB$ and $BA$.

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix}
3 \\
1 \\
1 \\
\end{pmatrix},
B = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & -1 & 2
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## Solution

$$
\begin{align}
AB &= \begin{pmatrix}
3 & -3 & 6 \\
1 & -1 & 2 \\
1 & -1 & 2
\end{pmatrix} \\
BA &= \begin{pmatrix}
4
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
