# Problem 1

Find the inverse of $\mathbf{A}$

$$
\begin{align}
A &= \begin{pmatrix}
4 & 0 & 5 \\
0 & 3 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & 4
\end{pmatrix}  \\
E_{1} = \begin{pmatrix}
\frac{1}{4} & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix} \to
A &= \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & \frac{5}{4} \\
0 & 3 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & 4
\end{pmatrix}  \\
E_{2} = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & \frac{1}{3} & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix} \to
A &= \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & \frac{5}{4} \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
1 & 0 & 4
\end{pmatrix}  \\
E_{3} = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
-1 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix} \to
A &= \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & \frac{5}{4} \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & \frac{11}{4}
\end{pmatrix} \\
E_{5} = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & \frac{4}{11}
\end{pmatrix} \to A &= \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & \frac{5}{4} \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix} \\
E_{6} = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & -\frac{5}{4} \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix} \to A &= \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
