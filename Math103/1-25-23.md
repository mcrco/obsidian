-4 & -3 & 0 \\

1 & 1 & -7 \\
2 & 1 & -14
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
\begin{align}
R_{3} \to R_{1} + 4R_{3}, R_{4} \to R_{4} - 2R_{3} &\implies \begin{pmatrix}
-4 & -3 & 0 \\
0 & -1 & 7 \\
0 & 1 & -28 \\
0 & -1 & 0
\end{pmatrix} \\
\end{align}
$$

They are linearly independent, none of them are multiples of one another and there are no solutions.
