$$
gcd(m,n) \cdot lcm(m,n) = mn
$$

1. (2,4): gcd = 2, lcm = 4, $2 \cdot 4 = 8$ 
2. $(3,7)$: gcd = 1, lcm = 21, $1 \cdot 21 = 3 \cdot 7 = 21$
3. $6,8$: gcd = 2, lcm = 24, $2 \cdot 18 = 6 \cdot 8 = 48$. 
4. $4,18$: gcd = 2, lcm = 36, $2 \cdot 36 = 4 \cdot 18= 72$
