# Discrete Structures

## P1

$$
a_{n} = 2a_{n-1} +8a_{n-2} + 81n^{2}
$$

Let 

$$
a_{n} = r^{n}
$$

Then

$$
\begin{align}
r^{n} &= 2r^{n-1}+8r^{n-2} \\
r^{n-2}(r^{2}-2r-8) &= 0 \\
r &= -2, 4
\end{align}
$$

So

$$
a_{n} = R(-2)^{n} + S(4)^{n}
$$

and 

$$
a_{n} = D_{1}n^{2}+D_{2}n+D_{3}
$$

## P2

$$
a_{n} = 4a_{n-1} - 3a_{n-2} + 20
$$

$$
\begin{align}
a_{n} &= 4a_{n-1} - 3a_{n-2} \\
r^{n-2}(r^{2}-4r+3) &= 0 \\
r &= 1, 3
\end{align}
$$

General solution must be

$$
\begin{align}
f(n) = Dn
\end{align}
$$
